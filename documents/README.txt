documents/

* 00_General_Information for Participants.pdf: sent to participants by email upon registration; describes the study procedure, duration, and compensation prior to the recording session.
* 01_Declaration_of_Consent.pdf: informed consent form, signed by participants Prior to participation.
* 02_Codeword_Instructions.pdf: instructions for generating a personal anonymization codeword, used to link participant data without storing identifying Information.
* 03_Baseline_Questionnaire.pdf: administered before the recording session; collects data such as handedness, sleep, caffeine intake, neurofeedback experience…
* 04_Experience Questionnair.pdf: administered just after the recording session; collects participants' subjective experience during the session.
* experiment_protocol.pdf: instructions for running the experiment: starting and ending a session, recording the different data streams, and verifying that the red noise is functioning correctly.
